Explaining the 2021–2024 Slowdown (+3pp Drag)
   Despite massive growth in registered mobile money accounts (e.g., Telebirr climbing past 54M) , actual account ownership grew by only 3 percentage points (from 46% to 49%).
     You can hypothesize and analyze several structural factors for this deceleration:
     
     High Overlap / Multi-Homing: Users who already held traditional bank accounts signed up for Telebirr/M-Pesa. This registered a massive jump in operator transactional records but zero net change in unique Global Findex survey account ownership (since they were already classified as "banked").  
     
     KYC and Fayda Hurdles: Stricter regulatory compliance limits unregistered wallet creation.  
     
     The Rural/Urban Divide: Digital wallet expansion remains highly concentrated in urban centers, while the vast, unbanked rural population remains disconnected due to infrastructure constraints.
